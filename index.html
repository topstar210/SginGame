Terms of Service | Odds Information

Before participating in the "DogeSpin.dog" game, it's important to understand the odds associated with different outcomes. The game operates with three rows and seven symbols, offering various possibilities. The odds of winning a basic prize, where any three of the six available symbols match, are approximately 1 in 2,929. Achieving a "Double Dogepot" win, which requires two consecutive "Dogepot" symbols in either the first two or last two rows, comes with odds of approximately 1 in 49. For the highest reward, the "Triple Dogepot" win, where all three rows match with the "7.png Dogepot" image, players need three consecutive "Dogepot" symbols, resulting in odds of roughly 1 in 5,439. It's important to note that these odds are approximate and based on the assumption of truly random symbol generation. In all other cases, where there are no matching or consecutive symbols, players do not win. Please be aware that participation in this game involves risk, and outcomes are determined by chance. Ensure you are familiar with the game's rules and potential outcomes before playing, and consider your risk tolerance accordingly. Play responsibly.
